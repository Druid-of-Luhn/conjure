~~> indicates output.

a repr rule is defined case by case + a prologue part.

the prologue part is like a heading for the whole rule.

=== prologue ===

will contain 2 or 3 outputs.
1st component: ~~> name of the representation
2nd component: ~~>  replacement domain
3rd component: ~~>  (optional) structural constraint    -- to be applied to each and every case.
4th component:      (optional) where/letting statements -- to be applied to each and every case.

== a case ==

starts with ***

1st component: a domain to match
2nd component: ~~>  (optional) structural constraint specific to this case.
4th component:      (optional) where/letting statements
